About Me

Hi! I'm Manuel, MSc student in Artificial Intelligence at Comillas Pontifical University (Madrid) with a BSc in Mathematical Engineering and AI. My journey academic journey has taken me from Madrid to Cornell University and Imperial College London, where I've explored different architectures in computer vision, robotics, and generative AI.

Only by deeply understanding the core foundations of today's technologies are we able to extract their full potential, optimize them, and build the future from there.

Beyond my professional interests, I enjoy fitness, nature, and reading. I consider these essential to living a balanced life, especially when shared with others.
